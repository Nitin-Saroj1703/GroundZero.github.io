About GROUND ZERO

An innovative solution for detecting and reporting road damage using AI technology

About the Application

GROUND ZERO is a web-based application designed to help identify, report, and manage road damage efficiently. Our system utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze images of road surfaces and automatically detect various types of damage including potholes, cracks, rutting, and edge damage.

The application provides a user-friendly interface for reporting road issues, visualizing damage locations on an interactive map, and tracking the status of reported problems. Municipalities and road maintenance agencies can use this tool to prioritize repairs and improve road safety.

Image Analysis

Upload images or use your device's camera to capture road damage for AI analysis

Interactive Mapping

View all reported damage on an interactive map with filtering capabilities

Damage Analytics

Track damage statistics and trends to prioritize maintenance efforts
