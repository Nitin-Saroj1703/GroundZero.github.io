Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the road damage detection?
Our AI-powered system has an accuracy rate of over 92% in detecting common road damages like potholes, cracks, and surface deterioration. Accuracy may vary based on image quality and lighting conditions.
How long does it take to process a report?
Typically, our system processes and analyzes reports within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email once your report has been reviewed and added to our database.
Can I use the mobile app without internet connection?
You can capture images without internet, but you'll need an active connection to submit reports and access most features of the application.
Is there a cost to use GROUND ZERO?
GROUND ZERO is completely free for citizens to report road damage. Municipalities and organizations may have subscription plans for advanced analytics and reporting tools.
